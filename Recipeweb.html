Bibimbap

Bibimbap (비빔밥) is probably one of the most well-known and beloved Korean dishes to many people. Even if people don’t know anything about Korea, it’s not too difficult to find people who had bibimbap sometime in their lives.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked short-grain rice
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1 cup julienned English cucumber
  • 1 cup julienned spinach
  • 1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced beef (optional, can be substituted with tofu or omitted for a vegetarian version)
  • Toasted sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Instructions For Bibimbap

Divide Rice: Divide the cooked rice evenly into serving bowls.

Arrange Vegetables and Beef: Arrange the cooked vegetables and beef (if using) in colorful sections on top of the rice. Place each type of vegetable and meat in its own section for visual appeal.

Add Egg: Place a fried egg on top of the vegetables in the center of each bowl.

Serve with Bibimbap Sauce: Serve the bibimbap with the prepared bibimbap sauce on the side or drizzle it over the top before serving.

Garnish: Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds over the top for added flavor and texture.

Mixing and Eating: To eat, mix everything together thoroughly with the bibimbap sauce. Enjoy the combination of flavors and textures!

Bibimbap Sauce

Ingredients

    Bibimbap Sauce
  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste).
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame oil.
  • 1/4 cup sugar.
  • 1/4 cup water.
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame seeds.
  • 4 tsp vinegar, apple cider vinegar or rice vinegar.
  • 4 tsp minced garlic.

Instructions For Bibimbap Sauce

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix them well.
